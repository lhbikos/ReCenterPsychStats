FAQ

How do I cite JASP?

One of the best ways that you can support the JASP project is by citing it. Citations are an important measure of how widely software is used, and an important indicator to funding bodies of JASPs relevance.

To cite JASP in publications use:

JASP Team (2022). JASP (Version 0.16.3)[Computer software].

And the BibTeX entry :

@MISC{JASP2022,
AUTHOR = {{JASP Team}},
TITLE = {{JASP (Version 0.16.3)[Computer software]}},
YEAR = {2022},
URL = {https://jasp-stats.org/}
}
